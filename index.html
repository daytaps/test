Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YUNI AI

What is YUNI AI?

YUNI AI is the first AI-powered meme coin ecosystem that combines artificial intelligence with community-driven tokenomics, offering unique features like AI-powered analytics, meme generation, and NFT creation.

How can I buy YUNI tokens?

You can buy YUNI tokens through PancakeSwap by connecting your wallet and swapping BNB for YUNI. Make sure to set the slippage to 12% to ensure your transaction goes through.

What are the staking rewards?

YUNI offers tiered staking rewards ranging from 15% to 40% APY depending on the staking duration and amount. Longer staking periods and higher amounts qualify for better rewards.

How does the AI integration work?

Our AI integration powers various features including market analysis, meme generation, and NFT creation. The AI models are trained on vast datasets to provide accurate predictions and creative outputs.
